Qualifying Life Events

Lose coverage? Move? Get married?  A qualifying life event is a life-changing situation – sometimes planned, sometimes unexpected – that allows you to enroll in health insurance.  Click here for a full list of qualifying life events.

Path to Pennie

Don’t have a qualifying life event?  Take the Path to Pennie  during tax season to get covered today.

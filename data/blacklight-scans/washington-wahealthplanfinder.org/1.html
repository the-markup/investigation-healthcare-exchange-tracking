How does it work?

Savings options

Washington Apple Health

Apple Health is the name for Medicaid in Washington. Apple Health is free or low-cost health and dental coverage for those who qualify.

You can apply for Apple Health at any time, and it is available year-round.

Exclusive savings

Tax credits and Cascade Care Savings reduce the amount you pay monthly. These savings are only available on Washington Healthplanfinder!

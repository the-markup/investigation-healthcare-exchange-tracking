Get Started

BeWell is here to connect you to health insurance if you do not qualify for Medicaid or have a health plan through your job. We are also the only place where you can qualify for subsidies to lower the cost of your monthly premiums.

Open Enrollment begins on November 1, 2025, and ends on January 15, 2026. You may be eligible for coverage now through a special enrollment period.

Find An Upcoming Event

Our BeWell team members are out in the community throughout the year. We regularly hold two types of events. At Enrollment events you can speak to a certified enrollment counselor who will help you enroll in a plan at the event. At Educational events you can get information about enrolling in health insurance through BeWell and the plans available. You can then enroll in a plan at a later time. Text JOIN to 844-515-5554 to get updates about BeWell events near you. Or find an event on our calendar and come see us!

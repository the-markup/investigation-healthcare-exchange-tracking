When can I buy insurance?

Between February 1 and October 31, individuals with a qualifying life event or who meet a certain income level can apply for coverage. Anyone can apply for coverage during the Open Enrollment Period, which runs November 1 – January 31.

Learn More
Where can I find my 1095-A form?

If you had a health plan through GetCoveredNJ in prior years, look for your Form 1095-A in the mail or your secure inbox in the January/February timeframe following your coverage year.

 

Learn More
How do I change my information?

Changes in income, residence, or household size can affect your eligibility for financial help. Update your information when a change happens.

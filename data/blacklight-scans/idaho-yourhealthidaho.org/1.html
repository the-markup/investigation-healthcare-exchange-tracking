WHAT IS YOUR HEALTH IDAHO?

Your Health Idaho is Idaho’s online marketplace where individuals and families can shop, compare, and choose health insurance from well-known medical and dental carriers. It is the only place Idahoans can apply for and receive a tax credit that acts as an instant discount to pay for some or all of their monthly health insurance premiums. Every plan offered through Your Health Idaho is required to cover ten essential health benefits, such as doctor visits, hospitalization, and preventive wellness.

PLANS TO MEET YOUR NEEDS

Idahoans who are not offered affordable employer-sponsored coverage or other government coverage, such as Medicaid, Medicare, or TRICARE, are eligible to shop for health insurance coverage through Your Health Idaho. With 154 medical and 21 dental plans to choose from, you’ll be sure to get the coverage you need. You can even use the plan comparison tool to make sure your doctor is in network and that your prescriptions will be covered in the plan you choose.

HAVE QUESTIONS?

We know that you may have questions when enrolling in coverage and ensuring you are utilizing all the benefits available to you. Help is available at NO COST through Your Health Idaho-certified agents and brokers. They are the local experts; here to answer your questions, walk you through the process, and pick a plan.
